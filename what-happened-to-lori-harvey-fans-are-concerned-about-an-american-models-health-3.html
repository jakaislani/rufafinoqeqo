What Happened To Lori Harvey? Fans Are Concerned About An American Models Health

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, May 18, 2024

American model and entrepreneur Lori Harvey has revealed shedding excess weight due to calorie deficit. What was her actual routine during the course? Netizens are curious to know about her journey.

The twenty-five-year model is also the founder of a skincare company named SKN by LH. Furthermore, she has also shown up in campaigns for Dolce and Gabbana, Michael Kors, Pat McGrath’s Beauty Lab, and Burberry.

Born on January 13, 1997, she is the daughter of Marjorie Harvey and the adoptive daughter of comedian Steve Harvey.

What Happened To Lori Harvey?

With the commitment of Lori to her boyfriend, Michael B. Jordan, she started gaining weight. According to news 24, she gained fifteen pounds.

The lady got worried about her health and the fitting of her clothes. Taking with the media, she said, “It was horrible; none of my clothes fit; it was just not OK.”

As a professional, she could not stand in front of the camera with considerable weight gain. Hence, she thought to ditch the weight.

Harvey has named her weight gain “relationship weight.” However, she is not reported to have any other medical issue behind the weight gain.

Back in college, she faced an injury that prevented her from continuing her work as an equestrian.

Lori Harvey Weight Loss Journey

Lori Harvey has spilled her secret about her weight loss journey in a TikTok video she posted on May 14. Disclosing her diet, the model said she ate twelve hundred calories maximum with meat and veggies.

Adding to the diet, she said that she had worked out five to six times a week where she often did pilates, sprint interval circuits, or hiking. Sometimes, she would repeat her exercise one more time.

However, the lady got criticism for her diet that eating such a low amount of calories would lead to malnutrition, eating disorders, mental health issues, and many other issues.

As said by insiders, the calorie she took for weight loss is not enough for a grown-up woman, instead is for a three-year-old.

In a remark in her video, she justified her diet, saying that the thing worked for her body. She said, “Before you all start your shit, this is what worked for me and MY body. everyone is different, so tailor your needs to meet your goals.”

Lori Harvey Health Update, Where Is She Now?

Lori Harvey seems completely fine as she recently glanced at Cannes Film Festival 2022. Her workout and diet routine has worked well on her body.

She was happy and excited to be on the looks of Bella. She attended the festival and the “Final cut” screen with Alexandre Vauthier wears and Messika Paris diamond jewelry. Maeve Reilly styled her looks.

Before Cannes, the model also had been to Met Gala, 2022, with her iconic looks and fashion.

The lady has been in her modeling career for more than seven years. She has signed contracts with renowned agencies like LA Model Management in the United States and Select Model Management in Europe.
