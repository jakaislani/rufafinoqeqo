Did Naomi Judd Kill Herself? Singers Cause Of Death Revealed

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

Fans of country music did not expect Naomi Judd; one-half of the Judds duo would kill herself. Instead, her daughter verified we lost her to the disease of mental illness. 

Naomi Judd’s family has just experienced a trade as she passed away at 76. Her daughter Wynonna and Ashely confirmed her death on Saturday as they verified on their Twitter statement to CNN.

Wynonna tweeted, ‘Today we experienced a tragedy. We lost our beautiful mother to the disease of mental illness. We are shattered.’

People Are Asking Did Naomi Judd Kill Herself? Learn The Cause Of Death Linked To Suicide

Naomi Judd’s cause of death has not been revealed, but when her daughter tweeted, we lost her to mental health, people asked, did she kill herself? She died on April 30, 2022, in the Nashville, Tennessee, region, 76. It seems she did not pass away from the natural causes.

How Many Children Did Naomi Judd Have? 

Naomi Judd was a mother of two, and her first child was Christina Ciminella, who is now renowned by Wynonna Judd. The artist gave birth when she was just 18 years old. Later, the singer became a mother to her second child Ashley, with her ex-husband Michael Ciminella. She raised her daughters as single parents.
