Lollipop Chainsaw Introduces The Bosses Of Zombie Rock

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 2, 2024

Recommended Videos

Lollipop Chainsaw looks like it’s going to be an insane amount of fun. Its latest trailer, detailing the “Bosses Of Zombie Rock,” gives us a chance to see what we’re going to be up against. I try not to get my hopes up, but if this is any indication of how the rest of the game is going to play out, I’m going to have warn loved ones that they may not hear from me for a while. Zed is standing out as my personal favorite right now, I simply knew too many people in high school that sounded just like him.
