What is the warm dry option on a dishwasher, and how does it work?

Heating drying is a feature in your dishwasher that uses pressurised hot air to transform moisture on dishes into steam, which then blows them out of the machine. This expedites drying and saves you the time and extra effort of cleaning away excess water after it has dried.

Aside from that, is there a reset button on the GE dishwasher?

During a cycle, hit the Start or Reset buttons on your GE dishwasher to restart it. For top-loading dishwashers, you’ll need to open the top, hit the button, and then close the top again. Then, wait for the dishwasher to pump out the water, which should take 2 minutes.

In this regard, what is the procedure for turning off a GE dishwasher?

Some vintage General Electric dishwashers are equipped with a rotary control knob. To cancel a cycle on these dishwashers, simply open the dishwasher door and the cycle will come to an end. Close the door after the water has stopped spraying and then reopen the door. In order to cancel the current cycle, slowly spin the knob clockwise until it reads “Off.”

Why are the lights on my dishwasher flashing?
