Namibia vs Papua New Guinea, 4th Match Prediction, Dream 11, Fantasy 11 Tips And Probable 11, Pitch

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 19, 2024

On November 23, 2022, at 1:00 PM IST, Namibia (NAM) and Papua New Guinea (PNG) will square off at the Wanderers Cricket Ground in Windhoek in the 106th match of the ICC CWC League 2, 2019-23. This is going to be the 5th time where the teams will face off with each other and give a thrilling match to catch upon.

As the ICC World Cup League 2 goes on, Namibia and Papua New Guinea (PNG) face one another. Namibia has a strong history versus PNG and has dominated this specific match for a while now. Also as we have seen previous matches we might know which is a more strong team, so let us check out the complete information about the match and other details that you need to know.

Namibia vs Papua New Guinea, 4th Match Prediction

The CWC League-2 One-Day points standings currently place Papua New Guinea at the bottom of the standings, while Namibia is currently ranked fifth.

Namibia has played twenty-two games so far this CWC League-2 one-Day season, winning thirteen of them, while Papua New Guinea has also played twenty-four games but has only been successful in winning two of them.

Prediction: Namibia to win this game.
