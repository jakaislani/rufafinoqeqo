Is Lateef Adedimeji Still Alive? Accident- What Happened To Him?

Lateef Adedimeji, the Nigerian Yoruba movie Actor, is alive and well in 2023. 

However, he was involved in a road accident in 2022, which caused rumors of his death to circulate on the internet.

Adedimeji confirmed the accident in an Instagram post, stating that he had a brake failure while driving but thankfully survived the incident.

In an Instagram post, Adedimeji expressed his gratitude to God for saving him from the accident due to a brake failure in his car.

The veteran Actor wrote, “May the Almighty be praised. Which of the blessings of God will I deny? You are indeed worthy of all my praise.

Thank you for your love, thank you for my family, thank you for my wife, thank you for saving me. That I escaped death a few days ago with the break failure is all by your grace; thank you, God, for always coming through for us. I will always praise you, Allah.”

A Look at the Career of Lateef Adedimeji

Lateef Adedimeji, a Nigerian Actor, filmmaker, and director, has become a household name in the Nigerian movie industry.

Having started his acting career in 2007, Adedimeji gained widespread recognition after his role in the movie “Kudi Klepto” in 2015.

He has since acted in over 100 Yoruba movies and has established himself as a prominent figure in the industry.

Aside from acting, Adedimeji is also a writer, producer, and director. He has written and produced several movies, including “Binta Ofege” and “Iyalenu.”

In 2020, he made his directorial debut with the movie “The Saviour,” which received positive reviews from critics and audiences.

Adedimeji’s acting skills earned him several awards, including the Best Supporting Actor (Yoruba) at the City People Movie Award in 2018 and the Best Actor in a Supporting Role at the Best of Nollywood Awards in 2019.

He is also an ambassador for popular brands such as MTN, Konga, and Harpic.
