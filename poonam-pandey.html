Poonam Pandey is an Indian model turn film actress and erotic artist, who has worked in several Bollywood films and Telugu cinema. She was born on 11 March 1991, in Kanpur, India. Currently, she resides in Mumbai, India.

Career Journey

She started her career as a model and walked for many Ramps of Indian fashion designers. Later on, she stepped to the acting and in 2013, she appeared in an Indian film Nasha, where she played the lead role of a seductress teacher. This film ends up with a sexual relationship with one of her students. The drama was very famous among the audience. She also signed in another film which is the sequel to Nasha as the role of Anita.

Next, she has appeared in the Telugu language film “Malini & Co.” alongside Samrat Reddy in 2015 as a lead actress. Her recent performance was a Hindi film “The Journey of Karma in 2018. In this film starred with Shakti Kapoor. The film is the story of a slum and genius girl who is good at studies and tries to c work in America.
